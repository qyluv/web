Valorant


MEET THE AGENTS

The game offers various characters to choose from, each with different abilities and can be used strategically to gain an advantage over your opponents. There are 4 types of Agents, each with their own unique purpose:

Duelist
Duelists are typically more aggressive and excel in direct combat. They are responsible for initiating engagements and getting entry kills when pushing into enemy territory.
Initiator
Initiators focus on disrupting enemy positions and gathering information on the enemy team. Initiators plays a crucial role to sow chaos and pressure on the enemy team by revealing their position for your team.
Controller
Controllers specialize in area denial and controlling the flow of the game.Often regarded as the control tower of the team, they possess abilities that cover large areas, obstruct vision, or control enemy movement.
Sentinel
Sentinels possess abilities that offer healing, utility, or enhance team survivability. They are responsible for holding defensive positions and anchoring sites during defensive rounds.


THE GAME

Valorant is a tactical first-person shooter (FPS) game developed by Riot Games. It features a unique blend of gunplay and strategic team-based gameplay. Players choose characters known as "Agents," each with special abilities that can be used strategically during matches.There are two sides: The Defender and Attacker where each team will play and changes side after 12 rounds. The Attacker needs to plant The Spike in the respective bomb sites and protect it until it explodes. Meanwhile The Defender side has to prevent The Attacker from planting The Spike or defuse The Spike before it explodes. The first team to win 13 rounds wins the game!
