SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS


Operating System: Android 4.1 or iOS 9.0
Processor: Quad-core processor (1.5 GHz or higher)
RAM: 2 GB
Graphics: Adreno 505 or equivalent (for Android)
Storage: 1 GB

  • Heroes

    • Player choose from a diverse roster of heroes, each with unique abilities and playstyles. There are tanks, fighters, mages, marksmen, assassins, and supports.

  • Map


    • The game is typically played on a map with three lanes: top, middle, and bottom. Each lane is defended by turrets and leads to the enemy base.

  • Minions


    • Both teams have waves of computer-controlled minions that automatically march towards the enemy base in each lane.

  • Turrets


    • Turrets defend each lane and must be destroyed to advance further into the enemy territory. Players must protect their turrets while attempting to destroy the enemy's.

  • Jungle


    • There is a jungle area between the lanes with neutral monsters. Players can defeat these monsters to gain experience, gold, and buffs.

THE GAME

If you dont possess any laptop or personal computer but itching to play popular MOBA titles such as LoL or DOTA, then Mobile Legends is for you. Mobile Legends is a popular multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game designed for mobile devices. Developed by Moonton, it features fast-paced 5v5 battles where players select unique heroes with diverse abilities, each fitting into specific roles like tank, marksman, mage, etc. The objective is to work collaboratively with teammates to destroy the enemy's base while defending one's own. The game is known for its accessible gameplay, intuitive controls, and dynamic team-based strategy. Mobile Legends has gained widespread popularity in the mobile gaming community, offering a compelling MOBA experience on handheld devices.
