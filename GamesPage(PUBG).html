THE GAME

PUBG short for (PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds) is a battle royale game where up to 100 players are dropped onto a large island map, and the last person or team standing wins. Players parachute onto the map and scavenge for weapons, equipment, and resources while staying within a shrinking play zone. The game combines survival and combat elements, emphasizing strategic positioning, gunplay, and adapting to the evolving map dynamics. Success in PUBG requires a blend of skill in shooting, stealth, and decision-making as players strive to be the last one standing in the ever-shrinking safe zone, known as the "circle." Teamwork, map awareness, and efficient looting contribute to securing the grand "Winner Winner Chicken Dinner!".







  • Zone Mechanics


    • The play area on the island shrinks over time due to a shrinking safe zone. Players caught outside the safe zone take damage over time. This mechanic forces players into closer proximity and intensifies the battles.

  • Combat


    • Combat in PUBG involves shooting and strategic play. Players engage in firefights with the goal of eliminating opponents. The game emphasizes realistic ballistics and bullet physics.
