Far Cry 6





SYSTEM REQUIREMENT


Operating System: Windows 10 (64-bit)
Processor: Intel Core i5-4460 or AMD Ryzen 3 1200
RAM: 8 GB
Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 960 or AMD Radeon R9 280X
Storage: 60 GB

THE GAME

Far Cry 6 is an open-world first-person shooter set on the fictional Caribbean island of Yara. Players take on the role of a guerrilla fighter attempting to overthrow the oppressive regime. The game features intense action, a gripping story, and a vast, dynamic open world.With an arsenal of weapons and the help of a variety of vehicles and allies, players engage in intense battles, guerrilla warfare, and face moral dilemmas in their quest to liberate Yara from oppression. Far Cry 6 promises a gripping narrative, diverse gameplay, and a visually immersive experience that has become a hallmark of the Far Cry series.
